- What is a [Dirichlet $L\dash$function](Dirichlet%20L%20function)?
  - Definition of a Dirichlet $L\dash$function:
  \[
  L(s ; \chi):=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{\chi(n)}{n^{s}}
  =
  =\prod_{p}\left(1-\chi(p) p^{-s}\right)^{-1}
  .\]
